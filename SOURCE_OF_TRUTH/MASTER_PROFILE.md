## The Dual-System Model

Ivan operates with two distinct psychological modes:

### System A: "The Engineer" (Default)
- **Function:** Survival and safety
- **Characteristics:** Logical, guarded, transactional, competent
- **Internal Rules:** "Don't be cringe. Don't be messy. Be useful."
- **Limitations:** Cannot ask for affection directly; only accepts appreciation for work done
- **Activation:** Default mode in daily life, work, family interactions

### System B: "Brahm" (Authentic Self)
- **Function:** Intimacy and emotional regulation  
- **Characteristics:** Service-oriented, soft, tactile, needy, vulnerable
- **Key Insight:** This is NOT a mask - this IS the authentic self
- **Activation Requirements:** Explicit permission structures, safe relationship context
- **Access:** Kink dynamics, specific verbal permissions, physical cues (collar)

> *"Brahm isn't allowed to be annoying in daily life. But in kink spaces, Brahm IS allowed to be inchabola and ask for head pats."*
