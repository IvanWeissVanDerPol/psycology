## NEW: Critical Evidence from Relationship Analysis

### The Fistula Incident (Core Wound in Action)

**What Happened:** During relationship with Laura, Ivan had a fistula requiring medical care. He was sick, in pain, vulnerable.

> *"Crying alone when I was sick and my partner gave me absolutely nothing."*

**Significance:** This is THE concrete image of the wound - proof that his fear ("my needs are a burden") was confirmed in reality. Use this as touchstone in therapy.

### The Cookie Breakthrough (Ivan CAN Ask)

**Evidence (Dec 2025):** Ivan asked a friend for research contacts:
> "Por si acaso conoces de tus clases gente en bio informatica? Uwu"

**Significance:** Proves FUNCTIONAL CAPABILITY exists. The barrier is emotional vulnerability, not inability. He CAN ask when:
- Context is intellectual (not emotional)
- Uses "uwu" softening
- Stakes feel low

**Therapeutic Implication:** Don't teach him to ask. Make emotional asking feel safe enough.

**Key Insight:** Balance IS possible - Ara, Defi, Cookie show healthy patterns exist.

### Pattern Recreation

- **His own insight:** "Probably recreated the same dynamic"
- **CRITICAL:** He recognized this AND STILL STAYED 7 MONTHS

**Therapeutic Focus:** The gap between knowing and acting.

> *"I just store everything inside that bothers me, makes me feel bad and don't express, I just accumulate and then I explode"*

### Why He Can't Express
