## Communication During Scenes

### Signals

| Signal | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Smiling** | I'm okay, keep going |
| **Face relaxed** | Genuinely content |
| **Puppy-dog eyebrows** | Something's wrong (even if saying fine) |
| **Fidgety/quiet without task** | May be freezing |
| **Smile gone + not responding** | Check in immediately |

### Check-In Methods

- **Squeeze hand:** Relaxed squeeze back = fine; fast/anxious = not okay
- **"Color?"** - Works without breaking space
- **Watch face** - Not good at hiding feelings

### May Go Slightly Nonverbal

Words get hard in deep subspace. Will still communicate through body and face.

## Recovery Protocol (If Triggered)

**Immediate:**
1. Stop scene (or continue gently if impact helps)
2. Physical containment - hold him
3. Warmth, pressure, wrapped up
4. Simple reassurance:
   - "I've got you"
   - "You're not too much"
   - "I want you here"

**Sequence:** Physical safety first, talk later when grounded.

> *"I don't need a lot of words in that moment. I need to be held until the bad feeling passes."*
