## ðŸŽª PHASE-SPECIFIC INTERVENTIONS

### Phase 1: Stabilization (Months 1-3)
**Focus:** Permission, self-notice, receiving

**Interventions:**
- Permission modeling: Explicit statements
- 3x daily check-ins: Body scan + emotion label
- Receiving practice: "Just sit with insight 30 seconds"

**Homework:**
- Track "uwu" usage in texts
- One "no-uwu" request per week
- Journal: "How am I feeling?" (not thinking)

---

### Phase 2: Deepening (Months 4-9)
**Focus:** Permission reduction, identity flexibility, non-kink vulnerability

**Interventions:**
- Empty chair: Engineer speaks to Brahm
- Values clarification: Is superiority a value or defense?
- Behavioral experiments: Meet competent domestic man
- Elicit Brahm: "What would Brahm say?"

**Homework:**
- Non-kink vulnerability practice (with safe friend)
- "I need..." statements without softening
- Service abstinence: Don't cook once

---

### Phase 3: Integration (Months 10-18)
**Focus:** Brahm accessibility, sustainable pacing, identity coherence

**Interventions:**
- Consolidation: Review journey
- Relapse prevention: Plan for stress reactivation
- Family strategy: If appropriate, gradual disclosure
- Termination planning: Transfer Brahm access internally

## ðŸ“‹ COMMON SESSION SCENARIOS

### Scenario 1: "I'm Fine" Mask
**Therapist:** "The Mask is saying you're fine. What does Brahm say?"
**Pause:** 10-15 seconds of silence
**Follow-up:** "Permission to not be fine. You can say 'I'm terrible.'"

### Scenario 2: Fixer Activation
**Ivan:** "You know, I could help you set up better scheduling software..."
**Therapist:** "That's The Fixer. I don't need help. What does Ivan need?"
**Redirect:** Back to his experience

### Scenario 3: Intellectualization Loop
**Ivan:** 5-minute analysis of relationship dynamics
**Therapist:** "Pause. Drop the analysis. What's the feeling?"
**Push:** "One word. Sad? Angry? Lonely?"

### Scenario 4: Permission Seeking
**Ivan:** "Would it be weird if I said I need a hug?"
**Therapist:** "You don't need permission here. Try again without the question."
**Model:** "I need a hug." (not a question)

### Scenario 5: Alexithymia Activation
**Ivan:** "I don't know how I feel"
**Therapist:** "Body scan. Shoulders? Chest? Stomach?"
**Prompt:** "Tight? Heavy? Empty? Tingling?"
**Bridge:** "Tight shoulders often mean... what for you?"
