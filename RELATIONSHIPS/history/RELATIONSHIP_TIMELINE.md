## Patterns Across Relationships

### Relationship Initiation

> *"Friend of a friend visits, cuddling, get horny, get romantic."*

- More passive/receptive in initiation
- Often starts casually, develops into more

### Selection Pattern

- May select emotionally unavailable partners
- Intellectual connection important
- Possible recreation of mother's unpredictability

### Communication Issues

- Partners may not meet emotional needs
- Subject doesn't communicate needs clearly
- Only tells partners about hurt if too painful to ignore
- Assumes partners should "just know" when visibly upset

### Service Pattern

- Service is primary expression across all relationships
- "The Fixer" operates in romantic contexts
- Gives more than asks for

### Staying Too Long

- Pattern of remaining in relationships past the expiration point
- Doesn't want to be "the bad guy"
- External circumstances used as reasons to stay

### Protective Mechanisms

- Can "kill feelings" deliberately
- Mask prevents full intimacy
- Need for partners to be self-sufficient (low demands)
