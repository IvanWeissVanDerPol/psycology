# Lucía Díaz - Relationship Analysis

> **Relationship Type:** Close Friend (Exchange Program / Becal)  
> **Time Period:** January 2023 - ongoing (Wichita State University exchange, continued in Paraguay)  
> **Chat Length:** 1900+ messages  
> **Status:** Active friendship

---

## Relationship Overview

Lucía is a close friend from Ivan's Becal scholarship exchange to Wichita State University, Kansas (early 2023). They were part of the same Paraguayan cohort studying abroad. The friendship continued after returning to Paraguay, with frequent visits, shared activities, and mutual support.

Key characteristics of this relationship:
- **Exchange program bonding** - Shared the Kansas experience
- **Mutual friend group** - Álvaro, Maia, Mauri, Yornely, Emilio, etc.
- **High warmth** - Terms of endearment ("baby", "gordi", "bebe")
- **Reciprocal care** - Unlike many other relationships, Lucía also provides for Ivan
- **Shared activities** - Movies, cooking, smoking, parties, gym, etc.
