## Therapeutic Significance

### Counter-Evidence for "Core Belief" Hypothesis

**Initial Hypothesis:**
Ivan believes at his core that he must "pay rent for existing" through usefulness.

**Alejandro as Counter-Evidence:**
If The Fixer were a CORE BELIEF, it would activate universally across ALL relationships. But it doesn't activate strongly with Alejandro.

**Revised Understanding:**
The Fixer is a **contextual defense mechanism** that activates MORE when:
- Connection feels precarious
- Proximity is limited (online/distance)
- Relationship lacks daily touchpoints
- Abandonment risk feels higher

The Fixer activates LESS when:
- Daily cohabitation provides consistent connection
- Relationship has deep history/foundation
- Presence alone feels sufficient

**Implication:**
Ivan's healing path may involve recognizing that The Fixer is a STRATEGY, not an IDENTITY. He doesn't need to be useful to exist - he already exists in Alejandro's life without performing.
