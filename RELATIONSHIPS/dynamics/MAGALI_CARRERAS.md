## Relationship Overview

Magali is a university friend from the pandemic era (2020-2021). She studies Aeronautical Engineering at UNA while Ivan studies Computer Science (Informatics). They share overlapping courses like Calculus (C2, C3), Linear Algebra, and Physics. The relationship is primarily characterized by Ivan providing extensive programming help while Magali reciprocates with exam materials and emotional warmth.

Key characteristics:
- **COVID-era friendship** - Formed during online university
- **Academic overlap** - Shared math/physics courses  
- **Extreme asymmetry** - Ivan provides hours of coding help
- **Warmth present** - Terms of endearment ("bb", "ami")
- **Gateway to friend groups** - Connected to Fran, Christian, study groups

When asked about his wellbeing:


### Low Emotional Reciprocity

Ivan shares very little personal information:
- Mentions moving out (Oct 2020) but frames it practically
- Jokes about "no daddy issues" (deflection)
- Never shares struggles, only projects/activities
- When injured (3/11/21), immediately minimizes: "Ya estoy bien xd"

## Psychological Insights

### The Fixer as Study Partner
This relationship shows The Fixer at its purest academic form:
- Ivan doesn't just help - he often does the entire assignment
- The pattern enabled Magali to pass programming despite hating it
- Ivan frames this as "no drama" - suggesting it costs him nothing

### One-Directional Emotional Flow
Magali freely shares:
- Breakup pain
- Bipolar disorder disclosure
- Academic stress
- Pet deaths

Ivan shares:
- Surface activities (cooking, projects)
- Jokes and memes
- Nothing emotionally vulnerable

### The "Chill" Mask
Ivan uses "chill" as both self-description and deflection:
- "Re chill" when discussing his own state
- "Chilli jaja" when agreeing to do her homework
- Creates impression nothing costs him effort

### Service as Emotional Connection
When Magali is hurting (breakup), Ivan's response sequence:
1. Brief emotional acknowledgment ("Necesitas un hug?")
2. Immediate pivot to practical help ("Te hago mientras el codigo")
3. Ongoing check-ins through service ("Avisa si necesitas mas help")

This matches the pattern identified in MASTER_PROFILE: **service is Ivan's primary language for connection**.
