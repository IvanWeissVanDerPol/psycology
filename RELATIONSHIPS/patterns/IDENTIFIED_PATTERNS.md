# Relationship Patterns: Analysis

> **Purpose:** Documented patterns that repeat across relationships  
> **Goal:** Awareness enables choice  
> **Last Updated:** January 2026 (11-profile analysis integrated)

---

## NEW: The Fixer Spectrum

Analysis of 11 relationships revealed The Fixer operates on a spectrum, not uniformly:

| Tier | Level | Relationships | Outcome |
|------|-------|---------------|---------|
| 1 | EXTREME | Laura, Magali | Depleting, one-way, crisis-level |
| 2 | HIGH | Rita, Lourdes, Jonatan | Significant service, some appreciation |
| 3 | MODERATE | Ara, Lucia, Ideline | More balanced, sustainable |
| 4 | LOW | Defi, Cookie | Healthy reciprocity |

**Key Insight:** Balance IS possible. Ivan CAN have relationships where The Fixer isn't extreme.

## Pattern 1: The Mask Prevents Depth

### How It Shows

- Presents "chill, successful guy" even in intimate relationships
- Partner may feel they don't fully know him
- Alexia ended it wanting "deeper connection" - the very thing he wanted too

### The Irony

> *"I was probably wearing the mask, being chill, not fully letting her in."*

### Impact

Prolonged suffering. Breaking point becomes crisis (fistula incident) rather than chosen exit.

### Therapeutic Implication

**Insight ≠ Action.** Ivan can intellectually identify patterns while emotionally remaining stuck. The gap between knowing and acting is a key treatment target.

## NEW: Pattern 3.5: Ivan CAN Ask for Help

### Evidence: Cookie Chat (Dec 2025)

> "Por si acaso conoces de tus clases gente en bio informatica? Uwu"

Ivan directly asked a friend for research contacts.

### What Made This Possible

- Context was intellectual (research), not emotional
- Used "uwu" softening (permission structure)
- Framed as "random question" (low stakes)
- Cookie was positioned as resource-holder, not caregiver

### Significance

**The capability exists.** Ivan is not functionally unable to ask - the barrier is emotional vulnerability. When asking feels safe enough, he can do it.

## Pattern 4: Service as Primary Expression

### How It Shows

- Cooks, cleans, massages, anticipates needs
- Present in every relationship
- "Gives more than asks for"

### The Deeper Layer

Service is love language AND:
- Earning right to exist
- Avoiding "pesado" label
- Creating safety through usefulness

### Risk

When unacknowledged, triggers the spiral. May be exhausting to maintain.

## Pattern 5: Communication Gaps

### What Goes Wrong

| Issue | Example |
|-------|---------|
| Doesn't express needs | Especially when feeling bad |
| Over-intellectualizes | Explains instead of feels |
| Avoids conflict | To keep peace |
| Assumes partner should know | When visibly upset |
| Only speaks up at breaking point | Minor hurts accumulate |

### The Paradox

EXCEPTIONAL written communication (Brahm letter) but difficulty with verbal/real-time expression.

### Implication

The capacity exists. The barrier is context, not capability.

## Pattern 6: Passive Initiation

### How It Shows

> *"Friend of a friend visits, cuddling, get horny, get romantic."*

- Relationships start casually
- More receptive than initiating
- Attraction develops through proximity

### Not Necessarily Negative

This is a valid way to form relationships. But worth noting if it means he's not actively seeking what he wants.

## Pattern 7: Selecting Emotionally Unavailable Partners

### Evidence

- Partners who don't meet emotional needs
- Laura: gave nothing during fistula incident
- Pattern of needs going unmet

### Question

Is this:
1. **Selection** - choosing unavailable partners?
2. **Communication** - not expressing needs clearly?
3. **Both** - selecting people who don't ask, then not volunteering?

Likely combination of all three.

### Cost

## NEW: Pattern 9: "Pesado" Prevention Strategy

### Evidence: Across Multiple Chats

Ivan apologizes for his presence before it's questioned:
- "Sorry if I'm too random sometimes" (Ideline)
- Hedges all requests
- Uses "uwu" to soften any potential imposition

### Function

Preemptive damage control. If he apologizes first, he can't be called "pesado."

### Impact

Reinforces the belief that his natural presence is potentially burdensome. Creates self-fulfilling cycle.

## Voice Note Evidence: Pattern Recreation

> **Source:** Voice notes from Jonatan chat during breakup processing (July 2025)  
> **Significance:** Ivan explicitly identifies recreating family dynamics

### The Mother Dynamic Recreation

From existing documentation:
> *"She was a bit meaner, had to adapt the whole house around her mood."*
> *"Walking on eggshells, moods I couldn't predict. Probably recreated the same dynamic [as with mother]."*

**PTT-20250729-WA0043.opus** (2025-07-29)
> "es una cosa un tofa que entra a mitad de casa, porque tipo no se, **mi vieja nunca me escuchaba, mi viejo es siempre de mandar y escomandar y decir como tiene que ser** tipo no se, en serio siento que **el dia de mis tromitas desde que yo saliera de expresarme y sentirme todo asi, que me caga**"

Ivan directly traces his communication difficulties to parental patterns:
- **Mother**: Never listened
- **Father**: Commanded, dictated how things should be
- **Result**: Ivan doesn't know how to express himself

### The Father Wound - Being Ignored

**PTT-20250730-WA0012.opus** (2025-07-30) - CRITICAL
> "es que no sé que mi padre... me vieron parecido **sentir mucho que esto de particular es algo malo es más hondo que me hicieron sentir un culo porque no me dieron porra no me hicieron sentir nada fucking ignore** [...] **john [father] su mundo es su pc el super el patio nada mas le importa y en eso nada mas vive** [...] **yo acepte lo que jamas seria good enough** tipo toda mi época de colegio hasta el ipt era porque no sos como tus hermanas"

This quote shows:
1. **Core wound**: Being made to feel like nothing, ignored
2. **Father's pattern**: Lives in his own world (PC, errands, yard)
3. **Core belief formed**: "I will never be good enough"
4. **Comparison to sisters**: Not measuring up

### Why Ivan Selects Emotionally Unavailable Partners

The voice notes reveal the mechanism:
- Father's emotional absence taught Ivan this is "normal"
- Mother's unpredictability taught him to adapt/walk on eggshells
- Laura recreated BOTH patterns: emotionally absent when needed (fistula), unpredictable moods

Ivan isn't randomly selecting unavailable partners - he's recreating what feels familiar.

### The Cycle - Ivan's Own Words

**PTT-20250729-WA0043.opus** (2025-07-29)
> "**yo soy nomas de guardar nomas dentro todo lo que me molesten, me hace sentir mal y no expresar, yo de juntar nomas y después exploto**"

This is the learned behavior from family:
1. **Store everything** (because expressing led to being ignored/scolded)
2. **Don't express** (expression was "pesado")
3. **Accumulate** (no safe outlet)
4. **Explode** (only breaking point permits expression)

### Evidence: Pattern Awareness ≠ Pattern Breaking

Ivan recognized the Laura-Mother parallel ("Probably recreated the same dynamic") but stayed 7 months after this insight. 

**Therapeutic implication:** Intellectual recognition is necessary but not sufficient. The patterns are survival adaptations that require more than awareness to change.
